Training Camps

Once per school term the 18 selected players will report for a week long training camp at Elliot High School. In that week they will go through a specialized training program designed to prepare them for life in professional soccer. In that week the CYC School of Excellence squad will play 2 friendlies against local Castle League teams (one division below Motsepe League). When the training camp ends the players will then go back to their respective local school teams and clubs to apply what they have learned at the camp.

Access to literature

The players will also be required to report to the resource centre for lectures on topics such as tactics, health, financial management etc. The content for the lectures will be prepared and sent to players email address (if registered with academy) to be viewed by the players at the CYC Resource Centre.

Coaching for coaches

One of the key components of the school development program is that all the school coaches will adopt one common coaching and playing philosophy that they will use to coach the players at their schools. That will mean that what I teach the players when they report for Soccer School of Excellence activities will be a continuation of what they have learned at school.

Tournaments and Tours

Once a year the CYC School of Excellence 18 man squad will go on a tour to Cape Town, Joburg or Durban to play against other Academies and/or play in tournaments. This will give the players good experience and exposure. During Easter Holidays there a lot of high profile tournaments in those cities. Taking part in high profile tournaments also serves as a platform for the players to market themselves in front of scouts from big teams looking for the next gem.

June Training Camp

June holidays we can have the first training camp. The squad will be made up of 18 of the most promising players in the school data base. I will train them and take them for the friendlies that week. I also want the training camp to serve as a purpose for the Elliot United FC coaches to see if there’s any players from the CYC Soccer School of Excellence that they would like to recruit to their team for the upcoming season.

September Schools Festival

The 24 September marks the day Batandwa Ndondo was killed by the police in 1985. CYC wants to use this occasion to host the school festival in commemoration of Batandwa Ndondo. It will be good education for the schools and the pupils as a whole because it’s important that they know their history and they must take pride in it.

December Training Camp

By the time this camp takes place a few players would have graduated and moved to Elliot United. Those positions in the Academy that have been vacated by the graduates will be filled up by the players scouted during the 24 September tournament. This camp will also serve as preparations for the 2017 Metropolitan/Bayhill Under 19 Premier Cup.
