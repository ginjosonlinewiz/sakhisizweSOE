Improve School Players Technique, Tactically, and Mentally

We aim to improve quality of soccer players at the local schools. Our focus is on the holistic development of the school player. Through our various programmes we improve the technique and tactical awareness of players by running professional drills.

Help local clubs reach the ultimate goal of playing in the PSL (South African Premier Soccer League)

A vibrant and competitive football school system will act as a role of a development structure, this leads to a pool highly trained football athletes for the local clubs to pick from, and these players will drive these local teams to the highest level of professional South African football.
